
HOW TO RUN:

- Install python 3.11 and add it to the system path.

- Enter "py -3 [path to the py file]" in the command line.
  Example: py -3 "C:\Users\example_username\eclipse-workspace\my_py_project\cs480lab2.py"

- Enter any infix math expression.
  The expression may contain the operators "+", "-", "*", "/", and "^".
  The expression may contain the functions sin, cos, tan, cot, ln, and log.
  The expression may contain parenthesis ("()") and curly brackets ("{}").

- To quit the program, enter "quit".
